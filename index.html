At Liberty Park Middle, we are committed to the middle school philosophy which embraces the unique characteristics of middle school students through grade level teaming, recognizing and teaching to various learning styles of our students, and providing all our students with the opportunity to excel in academics, athletics and fine arts. We have an outstanding faculty, fantastic students, and wonderfully supportive parents who have helped us create a physically safe and emotionally secure learning environment for our students. Liberty Park Middle strives to be a place that inspires enthusiasm, engages active participation, and encourages learning.
